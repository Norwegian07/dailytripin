NO.5 :- Panchava Waterfall (Pančavský vodopád)

HEIGHT :- 148 M.
At NO.5 we have the most beautiful and one of the highest waterfalls in Europe. In Europe it is loacted in Spindleruv Mlyn in the Hradec Králové Region. It flows more powerfully in the Spring season due to the melting of snow.

The whole waterfall can be seen from the blue tourist sign leading through the Elbe Mine. There is a hiking trail present between Labská bouda and Vrbaba bouda under the Pančavská known as Ambroz viewpoint. Where you can get a limited view of the waterfall.

NO.4 :- James Bruce Falls

HEIGHT :- 840 M.
At No.4 we have the 9th highest waterfall in the world and one of the highest measured waterfalls in North America. It is located in British Columbia, Canada. One of the major drawbacks of this waterfall is, it usually doesn't have a consistent flow throughout the year. So, they usually dry up in the summer season.

The only way to reach the waterfall is through Plane or Boat.

NO.3 :- Kynrem Falls

HEIGHT :- 305 M.
At NO.3 we have the 7th highest waterfall in INDIA, the Kynrem Falls. It is located in East Kashi hills in the Indian state of Meghalaya. It is situated inside the Thangkharang park. It is a three-tiered waterfall. We can get the best view of the waterfall from the Eco-Park located there.

The best time to visit the waterfall is in the Rainy season where we can see the gorgeous water cascading down the jagged rock face. We can go under the waterfall and enjoy the amazing falls, but be careful for time being. Rather from the view of the waterfall, you can get a view of Bangladesh too.

NO.2 :- Ribbon Fall

HEIGHT :- 491 M.
At NO.2 we have the highest waterfall in the USA. It is located in Yosemite National Park in California. It is the longest single-drop waterfall in North America. This fall is more active in the spring season. The deposit can reach a depth of 200 feet at the bottom.

It is also a seasonal waterfall-like Panchava Waterfall (Pančavský vodopád) which will dry in the summer season. There is an Ice cone present at the bottom of the waterfall.

NO.1 :- Kalandula Falls

HEIGHT :- 105 M.
At NO.1 we have one of the largest waterfalls by volume in Africa known as Kalandula Falls. It is located in the municipality of Calandula, Malanje Province, Angola. Its total width is about 400 M in total. Although the waterfalls outside Angola are not yet well known, they are among the most impressive natural wonders of Angola.

The saddest point of this waterfall is we can only see the top of the waterfall but we cannot reach the bottom of the waterfall.
