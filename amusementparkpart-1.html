It is a perfect place to enjoy with your Friends, Family, and if you are living in Germany and planning on a date it is a best place you got trust me. It has a Petting Zoo too beacuse in the beginning it started on the purpose of focusing on the animals. At last never miss the Acapulco divers show

Mango Meadows

At No.4 we have Mango Meadows Agriculural Theme Park which is not just an amusement park but World's first Agricultural theme park. It is located in Kaduthuruthy, Kerala, India. It was built on a 30 acres of land. It contains around 4800 species of plants, which includes 1900 species of medicinal plants, 700 species of tress, 900 species of flowering plants.
It is a place where you can relax and refresh your mind. If you are taking a break from your work and looking for a place to spend sometime this is the best place you got.
It is a best place to visit with FAMILY, FRIENDS, and best for chidrens. People Living in India must give it a try.
